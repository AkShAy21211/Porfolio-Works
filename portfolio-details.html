Project Overview

2.0 Project in Brief Aim: The primary objective is to develop an Intravenous Drip Monitoring System integrated with a pill dispenser. Objectives:
  • Reduce human effort in managing intravenous drips and medication schedules.
  • Designed for use in hospitals to enhance patient care and streamline nursing tasks.
  • Ensure patient safety by timely monitoring and dispensing medications.
 3.0 Project Target:
  • Create an efficient intravenous drip monitoring system.
  • Develop an automatic pill dispenser with precise dispensing capabilities.
  • Implement a system where the pill dispenser activates at the correct scheduled time with a buzzer and LED signal.
 Project Relevance:
  • Significant reduction in human effort, improving efficiency in medical care.
  • Enhanced patient safety through reliable intravenous drip monitoring and medication dispensing.
  • Easy-to-program and assemble system for user convenience.
  • Precise and accurate pill dispensing for optimal medication management.
